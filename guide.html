Resources & Guides

Discover a collection of guides, articles, and documentation created especially for international students. These resources are the result of real questions, requests, and research from students just like you.

Why are these resources important?

Studying abroad is exciting, but it comes with challenges: paperwork, cultural differences, finances, language barriers, and more. Our guides and articles exist to help you:

  • Save time by accessing reliable and practical information.
  • Avoid common mistakes that many new students face.
  • Feel supported and understood, even far from home.
  • Adapt more quickly to your new environment and thrive, not just survive.
